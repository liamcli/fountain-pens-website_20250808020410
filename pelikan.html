About Pelikan

Pelikan is a renowned German manufacturer that offers a diverse range of fountain pens celebrated for their craftsmanship and design. With a history dating back to 1838, Pelikan has established itself as one of the premier fountain pen makers in the world. Their pens are known for their distinctive striped barrels, high-quality materials, and exceptional writing experience. Whether you're a collector, enthusiast, or professional writer, Pelikan offers pens that combine functionality with timeless elegance.
