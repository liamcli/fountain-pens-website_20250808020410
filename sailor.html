About Sailor Fountain Pens

Sailor is renowned for its high-quality fountain pens, offering a range of models that cater to both enthusiasts and professionals. Founded in 1911 in Japan, Sailor has established itself as one of the premier fountain pen manufacturers in the world, known for their exceptional nibs and meticulous craftsmanship.

Popular Models

1911 Series

The 1911 series, named after the year Sailor was founded, features a classic cigar-shaped design and is available in multiple sizes:

  • 1911 Standard (1911S): Equipped with a 14K gold nib, this model offers a balanced writing experience.
  • 1911 Large (1911L): Slightly larger and fitted with a 21K gold nib, providing a more substantial feel.
  • 1911 King of Pens: The largest in the series, designed for those who prefer a more prominent pen.

Special editions, such as the "1911S Sparkle Cranberry," released as Sailor's 2021 Pen of the Year, feature unique designs like a translucent deep red body infused with shimmering silver particles.

Professional Gear (Pro Gear) Series

The Pro Gear series is distinguished by its flat-top design and is available in various sizes:

  • Pro Gear Standard: Features a 21K gold nib and a robust build.
  • Pro Gear Slim (also known as Sapporo): Comes with a 14K gold nib and a more compact size, suitable for those who prefer a lighter pen.
  • Pro Gear King of Pens: The largest in this series, offering a substantial writing instrument.

Notable editions include the "Pro Gear Slim Blue Dwarf," part of Sailor's Universe series, featuring a sparkling blue body inspired by blue dwarf stars.

Compass 1911

The Compass 1911 serves as an entry-level model, modeled after the 1911 series but offered at a more accessible price point. It features a transparent plastic body available in various colors and is equipped with a steel medium-fine nib. This model is ideal for those new to fountain pens or looking for a budget-friendly option.

Realo

The Realo is a piston-fill version of the 1911 series, allowing for a larger ink capacity. It maintains the classic design of the 1911 but includes an ink window to monitor ink levels.

ProColor

The ProColor series offers vibrant designs and is equipped with steel nibs, making it a more affordable option without compromising on quality. It's suitable for those seeking a reliable everyday pen with a touch of style.
