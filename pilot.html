Pilot Fountain Pens

Pilot Corporation is renowned for crafting exceptional fountain pens that blend Japanese craftsmanship with innovative design. From affordable entry-level options to premium luxury writing instruments, Pilot offers fountain pens for writers at every level. With a history dating back to 1918, Pilot has established itself as a leader in the writing instrument industry, known for smooth nibs, reliable ink flow, and thoughtful designs.

Popular Models

Pilot Metropolitan

An entry-level fountain pen known for its affordability and reliability. It features a brass body with a sleek design, making it a favorite among beginners.

The Metropolitan offers exceptional value with its smooth writing experience and professional appearance at an accessible price point.

Pilot Vanishing Point

This innovative pen boasts a retractable 18-karat gold nib, allowing for one-handed operation. Its unique design combines convenience with high-quality craftsmanship.

The Vanishing Point is perfect for professionals who need quick access to their writing instrument throughout the day.

Pilot Custom 74

A mid-range pen featuring a 14-karat gold nib and a transparent resin body, allowing users to monitor ink levels. It's appreciated for its smooth writing experience.

The Custom 74 represents the perfect step up for fountain pen enthusiasts looking to experience a gold nib.

Pilot Falcon

Known for its semi-flexible 14-karat gold nib, the Falcon allows for slight line variation, making it popular among calligraphers and those who enjoy expressive writing.

The unique nib design provides a writing experience that bridges the gap between modern and traditional fountain pens.
